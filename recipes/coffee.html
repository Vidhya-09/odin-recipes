Coffee

Description

Coffee is a beverage prepared from roasted coffee beans. Darkly colored, bitter, and slightly acidic, coffee has a stimulating effect on humans.

The combination of milk, coffee Powder, and sugar can be of multiple forms. White coffee is prepared using lightly roasted Arabica beans, which are generally roasted for just 15 minutes makes for a beautiful beverage.

Ingredients

  • Coffe Powder (Instant)
  • Milk
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Just add 1 tbsp coffee powder to a mug and add 3 tbsp hot water to it. Whisk well to combine the coffee with the water.
  2. Steam the milk now. You can use a French press to do so or an electric whisker.
  3. Now gently tilt the mug with the espresso shot and slowly add milk to it in circular motions. Add some sugar for taste. Once done, your Flat White is ready to be served.
