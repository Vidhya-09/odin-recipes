Cremebrulee

Description

Cremebrulee is a simple dish made of nothing more than 3 ingredients. It is as simple as egg sugar and vanilla essence. It is also known as burnt cream or Trinity cream, and virtually identical to the original crema catalana, is a dessert consisting of a rich custard base topped with a layer of hardened caramelized sugar.

It is a frencch classic that is a creamy, pudding-like, baked custard with a brittle top of melted sugar that cracks when you gently tap it with a spoon.

Ingredients

  • Egg
  • Sugar
  • Vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Crack the eggs nicely into a bowl
  2. Add sugar to the eggs and start whisking it until its all mixed and perfectly creamy
  3. Add vanilla extract to the mixture and mix it well
  4. Now place it in a water bath until it gains a pudding like consistency
  5. Add some caramalised sugar on top and its ready
